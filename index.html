AI Design Paradigm - RICH

We focus on developing AI design theory, iterating it across Ant Group's AI products, leading to the RICH design paradigm: Role, Intention, Conversation, and Hybrid UI.

AI Interface Solution

Building on the RICH paradigm and Ant Design language, the AGI Hybrid-UI solution blends GUI with natural conversation, presenting optimal components at each stage of human-computer interaction.

1.Awaken
Effortlessly Awaken ,   Instantly Engage
Helps new users quickly understand what AI can do, informs them of the AI’s capability range, and lowers entry barriers.
2.Express
Simple Express ,   Instant Feedback
Guides users on how to express intentions effectively, reducing misunderstandings with AI and clarifying their input.
3.Confirm
Process Confirm ,   Fully in Control
Keeps users informed of the AI’s task execution status, easing wait-time anxiety and providing a sense of control.
4.Feedback
Result Feedback ,   Built-in Trust
Clearly displays AI task completion, fostering trust, and enabling quick application of AI-generated results.
